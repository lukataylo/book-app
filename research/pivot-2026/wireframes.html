Pivot 2026 — wireframes

Three design directions, each covering the three new screens: Home, Memories (saved insights + spaced repetition), and the Learning tree (Duolingo-style). These are different information-architecture philosophies — pick a corner or hybridise. Low-fidelity on purpose: grey = placeholder, blue = primary action.

A · Catalog-first — Blinkist/Headway DNA (discovery leads) B · Habit-first — Duolingo DNA (the tree is home) C · Feed-first — Imprint DNA (swipeable illustrated feed leads)

Direction A — Catalog-first

Discovery feed is the front door, like Blinkist/Headway. Learning & memory live in tabs. Best for content browsing + conversion; lowest behaviour-change ambition.
